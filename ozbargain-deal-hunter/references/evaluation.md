# OzBargain Deal Evaluation

Use these rules when deciding what is a real bargain.

## Strong Positive Signals

- Clear all-time-low, historical-low, lowest-seen, price-match, clearance, stackable coupon, or unusually high percentage-off evidence.
- Net positive community votes relative to age, especially fast vote velocity in the first few hours.
- Comments confirm stock, checkout success, price match, cashback tracking, or model/spec accuracy.
- Final delivered price is competitive after shipping, membership, payment surcharge, or coupon conditions.
- Known Australian retailer, official brand store, or merchant with normal local warranty.
- Practical product with broad demand, not just a niche item discounted from an inflated RRP.

## Caution Signals

- Cashback is required for the headline price, especially if capped, new-customer-only, app-only, or tracking-sensitive.
- Targeted, invitation-only, first-order, student, business, subscription, membership, or state-limited conditions.
- Delivery, minimum spend, currency conversion, marketplace seller, grey import, refurbished, or warranty limitations materially change value.
- "Up to X% off" without a specific competitive SKU price.
- Low votes, many downvotes, or comments saying cheaper elsewhere, out of stock, price jacked, not the same model, or poor seller.
- Affiliate, Marketing, or Employee-tagged posts need extra verification rather than automatic rejection.

## Recommendation Threshold

Recommend only when at least two of these are true:

- community heat is strong for the deal age
- final price is clearly below normal market price
- comments support successful checkout or value
- seller and warranty risk are low
- conditions are simple enough for the user to actually obtain the price

If only one is true, place it in "Watch / Verify". If none are true, skip it even if the headline discount looks large.

## Verification Guidance

For high-value items, compare against the merchant page, one competitor, and a historical-price source when available. For cashback deals, verify cap, eligibility, tracking exclusions, and final effective price before recommending.
